Crafting Copywriting Gold with Advanced ChatGPT Prompts

Mar 28 8 min read

I recently turned a corner in my freelance writing career. It wasn’t a fancy software or new course that did it; it was a conversation with an AI named ChatGPT. For years, I’ve been writing copy that has been... nice. But nice doesn’t pay the bills. It’s time to get ruthlessly effective with our words.

Understanding the Power of Prompt Engineering

Believe me, you’ve seen mediocre copy. It's the kind that lands in your inbox with a subject line that screams “50% off” in all caps, or the one that starts mid-sentence and hopes you already know the product. Here's the thing: that’s because no one took the time to sit down with their AI tool and craft a solid, sales-boosting prompt. Yes, ChatGPT can write copy, but not all prompts are created equal.

Crafting High-Converting Copy Prompts

Let’s cut to the chase: a successful copy needs to meet three criteria – it has to be engaging, relatable, and clear. Crafting such prompts requires a deep understanding of your audience and a natural flow of language, which ChatGPT can emulate once you guide it correctly. Here’s a simple process:

  • Define Your Target Audience: Who are you selling to? Age, gender, income, interests – all these matter.
  • Identify Pain Points: What keeps your audience up at night? What do they want to escape?
  • Create a Persona: Bring your audience to life by creating characters. Let’s call our imaginary buyer “Samantha” – a 34-year-old yoga instructor living in Portland.

The Art of Conversational Copywriting

Remember, conversation is the art of not just talking, but listening too. I use this to my advantage when crafting prompt instructions for ChatGPT. I tell it to emulate a natural, two-way dialogue as if it was having a cup of coffee with Samantha. This results in copy that feels more personal and relatable, cutting through the noise of generic sales pitches.

Leverage the ‘Hook, Story, Offer’ Structure

One of the most effective copywriting structures is the ‘Hook, Story, Offer’ format. Start with a hook that grabs attention. Then weave a story that resonates with the reader. Finally, present an offer that feels like a natural extension of the narrative. When I direct ChatGPT, I structure my prompts around this simple yet powerful storytelling formula.

Real-World Example

A few weeks ago, I was tasked with writing an ad for a new line of eco-friendly yoga mats. I knew Samantha would love them. Here’s an approximation of what my prompt to ChatGPT would look like:

'Write an engaging ad for eco-conscious yoga enthusiasts, focusing on sustainability. Start with a powerful hook about protecting our planet, then share a relatable story of a yoga practitioner’s journey towards conscious living. Offer the new yoga mat as the missing link in their eco-friendly routine.'

And ChatGPT delivered. It crafted an ad that spoke to Samantha’s values and desires, making her more likely to click and convert.

Conclusion

Copywriting with ChatGPT is not about letting an AI do all the work; it’s about harnessing its capabilities with the right prompts. By learning the art of advanced ChatGPT prompt engineering, you can create copy that not only resonates with your audience but also leads to higher conversions. Try it out, and let me know how your sales soar.
