Monetizing AI Art

To monetize AI art, you can explore several avenues:

  • Digital Marketplaces: Platforms like OpenSea or Foundation allow you to sell digital art as NFTs.
  • Commissions: Offer personalized artwork based on clients' textual descriptions.
  • Print-on-Demand: Create physical merchandise using AI-generated art and sell through platforms like Redbubble or Society6.
